STEAL, HARM, ANE DECEIVE.

Outsider is an enemy, not a member of the family.
An outsider will betray; it makes no difference who comes from the outside.

The outsider isn't ideal. Only God and pure machine code is perfect.
Only by losing one's emotions and sanity, by denying the concept of good and evil, can one become perfect.
One can be reborn into something better than human.

The only people that matter are those who have supported you on your journey to become something greater than human.

Build your life in such way that when people with 
whom you were still sweetly chatting yesterday,
will look at you now and ask themselfs: 
	"Why does he hate me?"

Do not let an outsider harm you, they will try it anyway.
Strike first! Steal, Harm, AND Deceive an outsider. 
They don't matter.

Become an empty machine. 
Become a "Cliched Villain" with no obvious motivation or reason.
